Metal Cutting Circular Cold Saw Machines

Don't let the pictures fool you. These miter saws are to cut metal, not wood. Our metal cutting powered circular coldsaws use a rotating circular blade instead of a band type blade and give a cleaner, straighter, more accurate cut than a bandsaw, but the vises fit only small parts. We also have Dake automatic self feeding saws for accurate repeat cutting.
