Double Column Band Saw Machines:

When the head of a band sawing machine travels down on 2 posts, it is called a Dual Column type of bandsaw. This twin post design is great for cutting large parts while keeping the blade as straight as possible while the head is descending during the cut. This design keeps the blade from walking. They come in almost every size you could possibly want for cutting large pipe and structural shapes. Many options available. A swivel vise allows miter cutting. For frequent miter cutting we can build a saw with a special swivel head. If for some reason you dont see the twin post band saw you need, ask us to quote you a machine made special just for your work. We offer high quality quality Canadian made Verticut for those of you who are looking to save money to get one for a price that is less than an import. The WF Wells factory has been making their heavy duty horizontal band saw machine in the USA since 1949. The really big ones are built to order. Need one immediately? Many of our American made WF Wells and our imported Baileigh models are usually in stock.
